The Korean-Chinese boy band EXO is considered to be one of the biggest K-pop group in the industry not only because it is a 9-member band but because of its great global and international influence and popularity. They are known to be the Kings of K-pop. Because of the rapid rise of their popularity, EXO members have gained a massive number of fans all around the globe who are called the EXO-L.

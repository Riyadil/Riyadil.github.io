The discography of the South Korean-Chinese boy band Exo consists of seven studio albums, six extended plays, four live albums and twenty-eight singles. The group's first release, the extended play Mama, was released in April 2012. The album topped Korean music charts on its release. In 2018, Exo became "quintuple million sellers", meaning the band has sold over one million copies for five different albums. Since their debut in 2012, the group has sold 23.8 million downloads as of December 2017, and more than 11.9 million physical albums as of August 2019, in South Korea.

About EXO

EXO is a chinese-korean boy band under one of the biggest entertainment company of South Korea , SM entertainment , founded by Lee Sooman.It consists of 9 members , Suho , Lay , Baekhyun , Chen , Chanyeol , DO , Kai and Sehun.

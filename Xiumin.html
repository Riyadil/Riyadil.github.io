The Korean-Chinese boy band EXO is considered to be one of the biggest K-pop group in the industry not only because it is a 9-member band but because of its great global and international influence and popularity. They are known to be the Kings of K-pop. Because of the rapid rise of their popularity, EXO members have gained a massive number of fans all around the globe who are called the EXO-L.



XIUMIN

Stage Name: Xiumin (시우민)
Birth Name: Kim Min Seok (김민석)
Chinese Name: Jin Min Shuo (金珉碩)
Position: Sub Vocalist, Sub Rapper
Birthday: March 26, 1990
Zodiac Sign: Aries
Height: 172 cm (5’8″)
Weight: 57 kg (125 lbs)
Blood Type: B
Nationality: Korean
Super Power (Badge): Frost (Snowflake)

Xiumin Facts:

– Hometown: Guri, Gyeonggi province.
– Xiumin has a little sister whose identity he worked very hard to keep a secret.
– Education: Catholic Kwandong University.
– He became an SM trainee in 2008.
– He has a chubby face and a cute grin.
– Personality: Kind, very, very clean and tidy, cute, prankster, funny.
– His nicknames are Bao Zi (Little Bun), Mandoo, King of Detail.
– He is a good cook.
– Specialty: taekwondo, fencing, kendo, and soccer.
– Though his looks make him appear as the youngest member, he is actually the oldest.
– He is the neatest, strongest and cleanest member in EXO-M.
– According to Chanyeol, he seems cute but he’s actually manly (Star Show 360).
– He acted in the web dramas: “EXO Next Door” (2015), “Falling for Challenge” (2015)
– He acted in the movie “Seondal: The Man Who Sells the River” (2016)
– In 2017 he became a regular cast of the show “It’s Dangerous Beyond The Blankets”.
– He enlisted for the military on May 7, 2019.
– Xiumin’s Ideal Type: is someone who is hug-able and can give comfort to others.
